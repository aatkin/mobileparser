Macciavelli

Student restaurant Macciavelli serves up a variety of well-balanced lunch options every day. Take your pick from the student lunches on our menu or opt for a delicious restaurant-standard meal, which our chefs prepare at the grill at student prices. We now also offer a new, high protein salad lunch.

At our Take Away counter you can buy the lunch of the day, freshly baked bread, mouth-watering paninis, sweets, ice-cream, soft drinks and healthy snacks.

Macciavelli adapts perfectly to festive occasions for up to 260 people. For bigger events you can even hire the auditorium, the lobby and the sports hall. We also have a full licence to serve alcohol. What’s more, we prepare food to order according to our customers’ wishes, including sweet and savoury baked goods, sweet cakes and sandwich cakes as well as casseroles, soups and other warm meals.

We are based at the former army barracks at Educarium, the home of the Faculty of Education. Our restaurant offers a daily student lunch of healthy, well-balanced home cooking. At the deli counter you can have pizza, pasta, stir fries, or the steak or fish of the day cooked according to changing themes.
